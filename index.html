Study Design and Statistical Test Planner

This tool is designed to plan and evaluate your study for HCI research. Enter your independent and dependent variables, estimate the difference between extreme measures and their pooled standard deviation, and use the resulting guidance to prepare an appropriate design and statistical testing strategy. The planner runs its core power-related estimates directly in the browser using simulation-based approximations inspired by common ANOVA and regression workflows. It offers quick-start guidance and does not replace careful methodological planning or supervisory input.
